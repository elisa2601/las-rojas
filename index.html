AUTUMN

Autumn is a season with mild to cold weather so wearing a jacket is a necessity! In order to eliminate the clothes in the backpack is always handy to pack just one pair of pants that can be matched with all the outfits. Black jeans are the ideal ones for this season.

WINTER

Winter is known as the coldest season of the year, so being warm in the clothes that you wear is your most important goal. But winter clothes can be big and bulky so layering clothes is the most handfull lifehack we can give you.

SUMMER
